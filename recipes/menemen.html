Menemen

What's Menemen?

Menemen is a traditional Turkish dish made with eggs, tomatoes, and green peppers, cooked together in olive oil. It is commonly enjoyed for breakfast and served hot, often with fresh bread. Simple yet flavorful, menemen is a staple in Turkish cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 green peppers, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, grated or finely chopped
  • 3 eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes or paprika
    •

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped green peppers and cook until softened.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook until the mixture thickens slightly.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan.
  5. Gently stir or leave them slightly runny, depending on preference.
  6. Season with salt, black pepper, and optional spices.
  7. Cook until the eggs are just set, then remove from heat.
  8. Serve hot with fresh bread.
